#### 4.3 Five Cookie Categories (Interactive Toggles)

**Category 1: ESSENTIAL COOKIES** ✓ Always Active (Disabled Toggle)
- What they do:
  - Maintain your session and security
  - Remember your login information
  - Protect against fraud and abuse
  - Enable core website functionality
  - Save your preferences temporarily
- Cookie Duration:
  - Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  - Persistent Cookies: Remain for up to 1 year
- Examples:
  - Session ID (_session_id)
  - CSRF Protection (csrf_token)
  - Authentication (auth_token)

**Category 2: ANALYTICS COOKIES** (Optional Toggle)

#### 4.4 Quick Actions (4 Interactive Buttons)

1. **Accept All Cookies** - Accept all non-essential cookies
2. **Reject All (Non-Essential)** - Reject all non-essential cookies
3. **Save My Preferences** - Save your custom preferences
4. **Clear All Cookies** - Clear all cookies for this website

#### 4.5 How to Clear Cookies from Your Browser (4 Browsers)

**Google Chrome**
1. Click Settings (three dots) → Settings
2. Go to Privacy and security → Clear browsing data
3. Select "Cookies and other site data"
4. Choose time range and click Clear data

**Mozilla Firefox**
1. Click Menu (three lines) → Settings
2. Go to Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
3. Click "Clear Data"
4. Check "Cookies and Site Data" and click Clear

**Apple Safari**
1. Click Safari in menu bar → Preferences
2. Go to Privacy tab
3. Click "Manage Website Data"
4. Select sites and click Remove or Remove All

**Microsoft Edge**
1. Click Settings (three dots) → Settings
2. Go to Privacy → Clear browsing data
3. Select "Cookies and other site data"
4. Choose time range and click Clear now
