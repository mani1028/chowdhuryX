Flexible Engagement Models

Choose the right partnership model that aligns with your business needs

At ChowdhuryX, we understand that every business has unique requirements. We offer flexible engagement models designed to scale with your needs, whether you're a startup or enterprise.

Time & Materials

Pay only for work delivered with flexible scope adjustments.

Best For:

  • Discovery and experimental phases
  • Evolving requirements
  • Flexible timeline projects

Key Benefits:

  • Full transparency in billing
  • Quick iteration cycles
  • Flexible scope adjustments

Fixed Price

Predictable budget and timeline for well-defined projects.

Best For:

  • Website and app development
  • Feature implementations
  • Migration projects

Key Benefits:

  • Predictable budget and timeline
  • Clear acceptance criteria
  • Reduced financial risk

Retainer

Long-term partnership for ongoing support and continuous development.

Best For:

  • Ongoing maintenance and support
  • Continuous feature development
  • Long-term partnerships

Key Benefits:

  • Dedicated team available
  • Priority support and response
  • Cost predictability

Dedicated Team

Full-time team members working exclusively on your project.

Best For:

  • Product development
  • Scaling operations quickly
  • Multi-year projects

Key Benefits:

  • Exclusive team focus
  • Deep product understanding
  • Seamless integration

Staff Augmentation

Extend your team with specialized professionals for specific skills.

Best For:

  • Filling skill gaps
  • Handling peak workloads
  • Special expertise needs

Key Benefits:

  • Quick onboarding
  • Cost-effective scaling

Hybrid Engagement

Combine multiple models for your perfect engagement structure.

Best For:

  • Complex multi-phase projects

  • Growing companies

Key Benefits:

  • Maximum flexibility

  • Risk balanced engagement
