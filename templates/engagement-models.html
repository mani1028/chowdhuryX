Flexible Engagement Models

Choose the right partnership model that aligns with your business needs and growth objectives

At ChowdhuryX, we understand that every business has unique requirements. That's why we offer flexible engagement models designed to scale with your needs, whether you're a startup, growing company, or enterprise organization.

Key Benefits:

  • Pay only for work delivered
  • Flexible scope adjustments
  • Quick iteration cycles
  • Full transparency in billing

How It Works:

You pay based on the actual time spent and materials used. Our team works on your project with flexible hours and can pivot direction as needed. Perfect for discovery and experimental phases.

Starting at $50/hour

Fixed Price

Ideal for well-defined projects with clear scope and deliverables.

Best For:

  • Clearly defined project scope
  • Website and app development
  • Specific feature implementations
  • Migration projects

Key Benefits:

  • Predictable budget and timeline
  • Detailed specification required
  • Clear acceptance criteria
  • Reduced financial risk

How It Works:

We quote a fixed price for the entire project based on detailed specifications. You know the cost upfront, and we deliver within the agreed timeline and scope. Ideal for well-defined projects.

Based on Scope $10K - $500K+

Retainer

Long-term partnership for ongoing support, maintenance, and continuous development.

Best For:

  • Ongoing maintenance and support
  • Continuous feature development
  • Dedicated team engagement
  • Long-term partnerships

Key Benefits:

  • Dedicated team available
  • Priority support and response
  • Cost predictability
  • Flexible hour allocation

How It Works:

Secure a dedicated team or set number of hours monthly. Perfect for companies that need ongoing development, maintenance, and support. Your team becomes an extension of your organization.

Monthly Starting at $5,000

Dedicated Team

Full-time dedicated team members who work exclusively on your project.

Best For:

  • Product development companies
  • Scaling operations quickly
  • Building new teams
  • Multi-year projects

Key Benefits:

  • Exclusive team focus
  • Team building flexibility
  • Deep product understanding
  • Seamless integration

How It Works:

We assemble a dedicated team that works exclusively for you. You have direct management control, and the team integrates seamlessly with your organization. Ideal for scaling rapidly.

Per Team Member $3,000 - $8,000/mo

Staff Augmentation

Extend your team with specialized professionals for specific skills or capacity.

Best For:

  • Filling skill gaps
  • Handling peak workloads
  • Special expertise needs
  • Temporary capacity increase

Key Benefits:

  • Fill specific skill gaps
  • Quick onboarding
  • Cost-effective scaling
  • Your team manages them

How It Works:

We provide specialized professionals who report to your management. Ideal for handling workload spikes, acquiring specialized skills, or bridging knowledge gaps without permanent hiring.

Per Resource $2,500 - $7,000/mo

Hybrid Engagement

Combine multiple models to create the perfect engagement structure for your needs.

Best For:

  • Complex projects spanning phases
  • Uncertain requirements initially
  • Teams needing flexibility
  • Growing companies

Key Benefits:

  • Maximum flexibility
  • Customizable to your needs
  • Scalable approach
  • Risk balanced engagement

How It Works:

Combine Time & Materials for discovery with Fixed Price for execution, or mix Retainer with Staff Augmentation. We work with you to design the perfect model for your situation.
